EvolvingScholar · guided self-evolution

An AI Scholar Agent that evolves by mimicking a human learner.

Its growth is recorded as EPAs — Entrustable Professional Activities, a framework for credentialing competence — which it learns to apply.

#clinical-genetics #self-evolving-agent

the question, posed to a research intern on day one

"Review five years of literature on gene A and disease B, and study large real-world EMR cohorts — how far toward true expertise will that take you?"

How we learn — how the AI Scholar Agent evolves:

  • Run the project across many classic clinical-genetics diseases
  • Read the growth as a measurable trajectory
  • Not a claim of human-equivalent expertise

the idea

It learns the way a human learner does.

① Study

Validate known knowledge · discover new insights — literature tested against a real EMR cohort.

② Rounds

Method · result · questioning — presented to the PI, who gives structured feedback.

③ Grow

Feedback + self-reflection distilled into EPAs for future studies.

The human-learning currency: EPAs · skills · knowledge · lessons learned.
