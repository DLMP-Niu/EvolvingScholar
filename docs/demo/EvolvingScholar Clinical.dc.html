The trainee's transcript

One folder holds everything the intern has learned.

EvolvingScholar/
├── scholars/<id>/core/  the intern's evolving state
│ ├── persona.md   who it is
│ ├── goals/        research aims
│ ├── strategy/     how it designs questions
│ ├── concept_model/ its model of the disease
│ ├── capabilities/  ← earned EPAs
│ ├── knowledge/   validated facts
│ └── ledger/      mistakes & corrections
├── harness/     the mentor & evolve machinery
├── experiments/ cross-scholar results
├── data/        EMR cohort + literature
└── docs/        how we built it
The history of this folder is the revision map.

Every capability the intern earns is a versioned file. Reading its git history reads its growth — like a trainee's transcript over a rotation.

Learning vs. static

The only switch: whether the mentor is allowed to write new capabilities here. Nothing else changes.

And it noticed something

An intern that catches a coding error — and remembers it.

41 patients
